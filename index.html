CeTeau enables construction on weak soilsutilizing the best material and equipment in the world. Our core business is the manufacturing and installation of prefabricated vertical wick drains.

Core Products & Services

Ground Improvement Design

CeTeau provides ground breaking designs or value engineering for existing designs. Our dedicated in-house engineering team brings decades of experience to provide efficient design solutions.

Environmental Solutions

CeTeau provides tailor made solutions for erosion protection, water ponds, landfills and biogas. Our geomembranes and geosynthetic clay liners (GCL) are widely used for solid, liquid and gas applications.

Geosynthetic Manufacturing

CeTeau manufactures prefabricated vertical wick drains in The Netherlands, Malaysia, Mexico and Thailand. Our wick drains are made of high grade raw materials and are designed to provide a high discharge capacity during the entire consolidation process.

Contracting & Trading

CeTeau executes complete ground improvement and environmental projects. All geosynthetic products are manufactured or supplied to meet the project requirements.
